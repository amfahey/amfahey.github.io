About SLO Seltzer

SLO Seltzer was started by Abby Fahey, a Graphic Communication graduate from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo, in 2022. The brand started out as a senior project, and eventually grew into the company it is today.

The primary goal of SLO Seltzer was to create a yummy alcoholic beverage that felt more personalized and of a higher quality than the seltzers which were currently on the market. We strive to create unique packaging, delicious flavors, and a sense of community through our bevys.
