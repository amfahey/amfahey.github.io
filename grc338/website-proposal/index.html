Introduction

Abby Records

Abby Records is a small record company located in the heart of downtown San Luis Obispo, California. We sell a wide variety of products ranging from records and record players, to CDs and boomboxes, to band merchandise.

Target audience

The people who use this site are music lovers. Whatever era or genre of music you listen to, it can be found in some shape or form at Abby's Records. We attract as many demographics as possible by providing such a wide range of products.

The primary task of the people who use my website is most likely to shop for unique music products, either as a treat for themselves or a special gift for a friend or family member. A goal could be to find a specific record that they've been searching for for a while, or to browse albums and memorabilia from their favorite bands.

About

Abby's Records was started by Abby Fahey, a Graphic Communication student at California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo. As a lover of music herself, Abby grew up visiting dozens of record stores just because she loved the welcoming energy that they provided. Now, she is proud to own a record store of her own to provide the same environment for others to enjoy.
